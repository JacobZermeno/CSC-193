Rango is a 2011 American animated Western comedy film directed by Gore Verbinski and written by John Logan. The film stars Johnny Depp as Rango, with Isla Fisher, Abigail Breslin, Alfred Molina, Bill Nighy, Harry Dean Stanton, Ray Winstone and Timothy Olyphant in supporting roles. In the film, an anthropomorphic pet chameleon ends up in the town of Dirt, an outpost in the Mojave Desert that is in desperate need of a new sheriff. More about Rango.
